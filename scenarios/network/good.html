Resource Hints — Parallel Connection Setup

Preconnect to all cross-origin servers. Preload the LCP image at highest priority. DNS-prefetch for non-critical origins. Prefetch the next likely navigation. The browser's preload scanner does all the work before the page body even renders.

✅ What's fixed

  • preconnect to all cross-origin image/font servers in <head> — DNS+TCP+TLS runs in parallel with HTML parsing.
  • preload as="image" fetchpriority="high" on LCP hero — browser fetches it before parsing the body, LCP improves dramatically.
  • dns-prefetch for analytics — lighter than preconnect (DNS only), appropriate for non-critical origins.
  • prefetch for next page — loads in browser idle time; navigation appears instant.

📐 Before → After

  • No hints → 4× preconnect in <head>
  • LCP discovered late → preloaded at highest priority
  • ~400ms connection overhead → ~0ms (pre-warmed)
  • Next page cold → prefetched during idle time
